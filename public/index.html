Go Karting

A passion of mine has always been racing and go Karting is a hobby I enjoy. Karting is a very competitive sport and most profesionals have some background in go Karting. Karting is a sport you can get your whole family involved with. Kids can start racing in a club as young as four years old.

I don't have my own go kart any more so i join leauge night races at rental kart facilities. Its also a more budjet friendly option and it saves me a lot of time from regular maintaince.

additional things you'll need

  • Helmet
  • Racing suit
  • fire resistant shoes
  • Neck harness
  • Rib vest
  • fire resistant Gloves
